About me!

Hi! I'm Madeline, a master's student in Digital Archaeology at the University of York (UK). I hold a Bachelor of Science in Archaeological Studies from the University of Wisconsin-La Crosse (USA) with minors in History, Earth Science, and Geoarchaeology, plus a Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Certificate. I'm passionate about using geospatial technologies to address real-world challenges in archaeology, environmental sciences, and beyond. Explore this website to learn more about what I do!
